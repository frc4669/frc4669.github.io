The concept for Voiceless was founded off the general need for such a documentary and project. We all noticed that there was a problem with the way that homelessness was being handled and how there are very harsh stereotypes and stigmas that face the homeless. Being youth, we decided to create a project that would shine light onto the very big problem that is youth homelessness. The goal of â€œVoicelessâ€ is to deliver a presentation via video documentary that aims to dispel the stereotypes that homeless face, while shining light on the greater problem that faces our city. With Voiceless, the aim is also to shine light onto the true population that does not have a voice; that being the homeless youth population. This project is being sponsored by a $5,000 grant given to us by Bling. The video project will be comprised of multiple interview clips from various homeless people. We aim to film and record interviews and upload some of them to our social media pages; YouTube and Instagram. The documentary aspect of our project will come in the form of an hour long film that is a combination of the interviews and us helping out the homeless. With this project, we are not aiming to highlight the style or technique of video that we will be using. Rather, it would highlight the human nature of the people behind the camera, which is a part that we believe has been left out. We would greatly appreciate any help in the form of letting us use your shelter or space to film and interview the homeless. Each interview would be personal and every interview would not use the exact same questions. The aim of the interview is to find a common ground between the homeless person and learn about their story and their pathway to where they are right now.
